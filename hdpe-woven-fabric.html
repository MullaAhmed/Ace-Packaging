HDPE Woven Fabrics

Laminated PP/HDPE Woven Fabrics are used to protect apparels, bags, beds, etc. from dust and other contaminants. Laminated polypropylene woven fabrics safeguard the covered material from external elements which can damage the quality and glaze. Laminated pp fabric also adds durability to and sustains the quality of protected materials. 



Specification-

  • GSM- 50 GSM to 270 GSM
  • Size- 30cm to 200cm
  • PP/HDPE fabric- coated, uncoated, reinforced for tubular bags
  • UV Stabilisation- yes, as per the requiremenst
